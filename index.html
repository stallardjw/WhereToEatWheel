🍪 This site stores a small cookie on your device to remember your restaurant list and preferences. No personal data is collected, no tracking, no third parties.

Cookie & Storage Policy

This site uses one functional cookie and localStorage to save your personal list of restaurants and theme preference between visits.

What we store

  • weating_uid (cookie) — A randomly generated ID unique to your browser. This is not linked to any personal information.
  • weating_[uid]_restaurants (localStorage) — Your saved restaurant list.
  • weating_[uid]_theme (localStorage) — Your light/dark theme preference.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
  • We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.
  • We do not track your location or browsing behaviour.

Your rights

You can decline cookies at any time. If you decline, nothing is saved and your list will reset on each visit. You can also clear all stored data using the Reset button in the app.

Location access (used by the "Find Nearby" feature) is requested separately by your browser and is never stored by this site.

Clear the wheel?

This will remove all restaurants from your list. This cannot be undone.
